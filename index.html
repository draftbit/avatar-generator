Personas by Draftbit — Avatar Generator

A playful avatar generator for the modern age. Mix and match skin, hair, facial hair, body, eyes, mouth, nose, and accessories with customizable colors, then download your avatar as a PNG or share it as a URL.

Every avatar is a link: compose one with query parameters like ?hair=Mohawk&hairColor=F2C94C&eyes=Hearts, fetch it as an image from /api/avatar.svg, discover valid options at /api/options, or connect via the MCP server at /mcp. Details: llms.txt.
